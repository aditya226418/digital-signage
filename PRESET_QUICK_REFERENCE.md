## üéØ Choosing the Right Preset

### By Restaurant Type:
- **Fine Dining:** Elegant, Luxury, Classic
- **Casual Dining:** Rustic, Cafe, Vintage
- **Fast Casual:** Modern, Street Food, Bold
- **Healthy/Organic:** Fresh, Minimal, Chalkboard
- **Specialty:** Ocean (seafood), Cafe (coffee)

### By Color Preference:
- **Warm Tones:** Classic, Rustic, Vintage, Cafe
- **Cool Tones:** Ocean, Fresh, Minimal
- **Dark Themes:** Modern, Luxury, Chalkboard
- **Bright/Bold:** Bold, Street Food, Fresh

### By Font Style:
- **Serif (Elegant):** Classic, Elegant, Rustic, Luxury, Cafe
- **Sans-Serif (Modern):** Modern, Minimal, Fresh, Street Food, Ocean, Vintage
- **Handwriting:** Chalkboard, Bold
