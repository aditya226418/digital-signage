╔════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                          STEP INDICATOR STATES                                  ║
╚════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Completed Steps:     [✓] Green circle with checkmark (opacity-60)
Current Step:        [●] Primary color circle with step number
Future Steps:        [ ] Muted circle with step number (opacity-40)

Users can click on completed steps to navigate backward.


╔════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                      RESPONSIVE OVERFLOW HANDLING                              ║
╚════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Sidebar:          overflow-y-auto (scrolls vertically if needed)
Main Content:     overflow-y-auto (scrolls vertically if needed)
Container:        overflow-hidden (prevents outer scroll)

This ensures both areas scroll independently without creating nested scrollbars.
